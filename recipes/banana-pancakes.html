Banana Pancakes

Description:

Warm, fluffy and yummy are just a few ways to describe this delicious treat. With only 5 minutes prep time and 10 minutes cooktime you'd be hard pressed to find a more simple breakfast that tastes so freaking good!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/4 cup teaspoon salt
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 ripe bananas, mashed
  • 1/4 cup pecans (optional)
  • 1 banana, sliced (optional)
  • whipped cream (optional)

Steps

  1. Combine flour, white sugar, baking powder and salt.
  2. In a seperate bowl, mix together egg, milk, vegetable oil and mashed bananas.
  3. Stir flour mixture into banana mixture; batter will be slightly lumpy.
  4. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium high heat.
  5. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake.
  6. Cook until pancakes are golden brown on botht sides.
  7. Sprinkle with pecans, top with bananas and whipped cream. (optional)
  8. Serve.
