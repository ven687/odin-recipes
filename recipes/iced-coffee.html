Chocolate Iced Coffee

Description:

A delicious and refreshing treat for chocolatiers and coffee-lovers alike. It only takes two minutes to prepare and serves two. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 1 half cup warm water
  • 2 teaspoons instant coffee granules
  • 1 tray of ice cubes
  • 1 half (5 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 half cup of milk
  • 1 tablespoon chocolate syrup

Steps

  1. In a small bowl, stir the water and instant coffee together.
  2. In a blender, combine ice cubes, coffee mixture, milk, sweetened condensed milk and chocolate syrup.
  3. Blend until smooth.
  4. Pour into glasses and serve
