Maple Salmon

Description:

A simple yet delicous way to spice up your salmon! Only takes one hour and serves up to 4!

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 pound salmon
  • 1 pinch cilantro (optional)

Steps

  1. In a small bowl, mix the maple syrup, soy sauce, garlic, garlic salt and pepper.
  2. Place salmon in a shallow glass baking dish, and coat with maple syrup mixture.
  3. Cover the dish and marinate salmon in refrigerator for 30 minutes, turning once.
  4. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
  5. Place baking dish in oven and bake uncovered for 20 minutes, or until easily flaked with a fork.
  6. Top with cilantro (optional)
  7. Serve.
